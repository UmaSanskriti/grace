Questions families ask

What happens on the first call?

A calm conversation, not a script. Grace asks what your family needs, repeats it back — and nothing is shared until you approve it.

We’re on a deadline. Can Grace keep up?

Yes — mention the deadline first. Grace prioritizes homes that can meet it, and is honest when a timeline looks tight.

Do we have to talk about money?

Only as much as you want. Grace gathers itemized prices for clarity — you’ll never be asked to name a budget.

Who sees our information?

Only the funeral homes you approve, only the details you’ve confirmed. Ask anytime what’s been shared, or reply STOP to any text to end contact.

What does talking to Grace cost?

Nothing. Funeral homes don’t pay Grace and she takes no commissions.
